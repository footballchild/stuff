A Refreshing Start

We are so sorry for the devastation that the typhoon has caused you. We hope that during all of this time of pain, you will find a light, a light that will comfort you and help you through all this pain. Hundreds and even thousands of people want to help you and among those people are the OB Montessori family.


OB Montessori Center is willing to help you in everything you need. I hope our prayers and our messages of hope finds all who have been affected. We hope that the indominable spirit that always keeps Filipinos in a positive mood is still there, and even if it is gone, we hope it will find its way back to you again. The spirit that no matter what happens, Filipinos will never give up and will always fight the pain! We hope you find courage that will help you overcome this crisis just as you have overcome every other pain in your life.


Not only OBMC, but everyone prays and hopes for the speedy recovery for all affected by the typhoon.
